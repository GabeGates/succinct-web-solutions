Who we are

Succinct Web Solutions is a Canadian business started in the rural community of Belleisle Creek, New Brunswick. We have since moved operations to Hampton where we can also serve the greater Saint John area. Founded in 2017, we provide responsive, high performance, web-based solutions for everyone. Being a small business we take extra care and responsibility for everything we publish. We are always researching the latest technologies and design patterns to give the best service back to our clients.

Gabriel Gates Founder/Operator

Gabe spent the first 10 years of his work experience publishing enterprise applications on the web within the pharmaceutical industry. As a full-stack developer, he is very passionate about learning and using new technologies. Most of his work experience has been a mix of frontend and backend development using technologies like Java, HTML, Sass, Angular, React, Spring, Docker, MySQL, and Serverless microservices.

Gabe received his Computer Programming Technology - Cooperative Education diploma from the New Brunswick Community College in 2007. As part of the program, he was handpicked for a 6-week work term in Cienfuegos, Cuba. He was also selected for a 6-month placement for the Department of Education, which was extended for the full year. He continued gaining work experience where he became a Senior Developer for Elandas in Saint John, New Brunswick.
